What is Youtube Analytics

Youtube analytics is a feature provided that allows content creators to track or monitor the data it has gathered from their channel, taking the data that it has collected and forming them into proper statistics labeled for the demographics and the type of viewer you have.







In Summary

Youtube analytics is a tool provided for content creators so they can monitor and learn the data from the videos they had made. This video tackles how to use the analytics, what you'll see when you use it, and the benefits.
